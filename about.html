Med.AI was built to solve the "Information Overload" crisis in healthcare by leveraging cutting-edge NLP to distill complex documents into actionable intelligence.

The Challenge

Drowning in Data

Healthcare professionals spend 30% of their time reviewing administrative documents. Policy briefs are dense, verbose, and difficult to parse quickly.

  • Critical updates buried in 50+ page PDFs.
  • High risk of missing compliance changes.
  • Manual summarization is prone to human error.

Workflow

From Document to Decision

1. Upload & Parse

Users upload PDF/DOCX files. Our system securely parses raw text, maintaining structural integrity via OCR/Text extraction.

1
2

2. AI Processing

We apply BERTSumExt to score sentences based on importance and SBERT to cluster thematic topics automatically.

3. Insight Generation

A concise summary is generated. The Gemini API then acts as a reasoning engine to answer specific Q&A.

Vocabulary

Tech Glossary

NLP

Natural Language Processing. A field of AI giving machines the ability to understand text.

BERTSumExt

A BERT variation fine-tuned for Extractive Summarization, selecting exact source sentences.

KMeans

Unsupervised clustering algorithm used to group similar sentences by theme.

Gemini

Google's multimodal generative AI, used here for reasoning and Q&A tasks.
